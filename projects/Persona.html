Persona by Sense

[2020-21]  [Speculative-Design]

What if our inner emotions were visible to other people? Let's imagine a world in which all the social masks are cancelled, in which there's no possibility to lie or hide our feelings. This is possible thanks to a physical kit and an emotion detection software capable of revealing our deepest emotions and predict future developments of our personal relationships.
