Archive & the City

[2025]  [Motion Design]

Archive & the City is a special project inside the Biennale Architettura 2025 curated by Carlo Ratti. The exhibition explores the link between archives and cities, two worlds apparently far apart, but with surprising similarities. In this context, I worked on an immersive room with video projection on the walls showing historical items and data visualizations about the Biennale archive.

Curated by: Carlo Ratti
Concept and exhibition design: Studio FM Milano
Creative direction and graphic design: Studio FM Milano
Data visualization: Density Design Lab – Politecnico di Milano
Theoretical contributions: Jon Kleinberg, Marco Santambrogio, Achille Varzi, Historical Archive of La Biennale di Venezia (ASAC)
Video: Leonardo Puca
Type design: Stefano Cremisini – CAST Foundry
