Psicologi Palasport 2025

[2025]  [Visual Art Direction]

For the live show of Psicologi I was in charge from Galattico Studio to be the art director of the visuals. The overall mood takes inspiration from a digital aesthetic heavily influenced by the internet core: ranging from social network, digital interfaces, memes, videogames, low-fi content. During the concert we developed several moments in which an AI Avatar breaks in and speaks directly to the public creating a narrative flow during the entire show.

Creative Director: Filippo Rossi
Motion: Jolanda Fiaré, Simone Inverardi, Giovanni Migliorucci, Tania Zama
Producer: Tommaso Bignù
