Cuphead Sneakers

[2019]  [3D Modeling]

A personal experiment in 3D modeling based on a fictional collaboration between Nike, Off-White and the video game Cuphead. The shape and color of the sneakers recall the characters from the video game that are inspired by the Rubber Hose Animation style, typical of 1940s cartoons and animated films.
