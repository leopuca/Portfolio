3D-DVD-logo

[2023]  [Creative Coding]

In this coding experiment I hacked the iconic DVD logo animation by making it 3d and more physically accurate. By combining Three.js and Cannon.js I was able to render a physic simulation on the web in real time. Also you can make a mess with the scene by adding as many DVDs as you wish.
