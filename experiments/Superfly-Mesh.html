Superfly Mesh

[2021]  [Photogrammetry | 3D Motion]

3D animation created from the photogrammetry of a track and field shoe. In the video, the shoe is multiplied many times, creating a composition that resembles the movement of running. The lighting of the scene allows to emphasize the organic shapes of the sole that reminds the craters of a celestial body.
